About Me

My name is Sean Brownlee, and I am a senior studying Games and Interactive Media. I have always had a strong passion for video games and enjoy playing them in my free time.

Video games have been a huge part of my life, and they continue to inspire my academic and career goals. I enjoy exploring different genres and understanding what makes games engaging and how are games made.

Why I Love Video Games

I chose to focus on video games because they are something I truly enjoy. I play games daily and appreciate the variety of experiences they offer.

This project helped me reflect on my favorite games and better understand what makes them meaningful to me.

How I Chose My Top 5

The way I picked my top 5 was I looked on my xbox at the games that I had, and I looked and play time and kind of nostalgia of my time playing the game when I was.

By looking at some games that I haven't played in a while I noticed that it was way harder to make the list, As I was rembering having a great time playing a lot of games and logged tons of hours on them.

But overall I finally stuck to 5 games that made the most impact and consumed the most time in my life.

What Makes a Good Game?

For me, a good game is one that keeps my attention. If I’m not bored and always want to keep playing, then the game is successful.

Games that offer strong gameplay, replayability, and engaging mechanics are the ones I enjoy the most. I get bored or side tracked pretty easy and so if a game is able to attract my attention for a long period of time and keep me wanting to play more and play everyday, thats when I know I have found a good game for me.
